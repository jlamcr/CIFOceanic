    About Us

    CIF Oceanic Trading was born in response to the need to provide a quality service and above all security, in the framework of negotiations with China and its exports to the Hispanic world mainly.

    We have human resources specialized in international trade, with absolute command of the Mandarin language. We speak Spanish with our clients and Mandarin with our suppliers, which generates a more efficient comprehensive communication network.

    The first Oceanic office was created in Zhejiang province, in the well-known city of Yiwu (the world's largest commodities market) at the end of 2014. As of today, CIF Oceanic has offices in two more cities in China (Hangzhou and Hainan) and our recent opening in 2020 in San José Costa Rica.

    We firmly believe in the economic growth of our clients working in a format of cooperation and mutual trust.

    "We speak Spanish with our clients and Mandarin with our suppliers, thus avoiding communication problems in the import process."

    Our Services

    CIF Oceanic is an officially registered company in a special development zone, designated as a free trade port.

    Search for Products and Suppliers

    To search for suppliers we go to various sources, some of these are:

    Acquaintances: people who can recommend suppliers to us, either because they know them when they are or have been in businesses similar to ours, or for any other reason.

    Company Workers: Workers who are likely to know suppliers they have previously worked with in their former jobs.

    Competition: competing companies that we can investigate to find out who their suppliers are.

    Alibaba: perhaps the most popular tool in the world and for importers outside of China it represents the only search option. Alibaba helps us as a directory to locate suppliers by region (the Chinese territory is very extensive) but it is not our only criteria for selecting suppliers.

    Trade Shows or Specialized Exhibitions: We are part of the foreign exporters chamber in China. We frequently attend trade fairs, exhibitions and specialized conferences in order to expand our network of suppliers and consolidate business relationships.

    The selection criteria are determined by the client

    Price: the price of your products, the expenses that could be added to it, the discounts that you could grant us.

    Quality: the quality of the supplies, products or services offered. It is recommended to send sample to customer.

    Payment term: the payment conditions that they provide us, if they ask us to pay in cash, or give us facilities to pay on credit.

    Guarantees: the guarantees granted and the duration of these.

    Reputation: the prestige and good references you have.

    Quality Control

    Documentation: We verify that the supplier has the necessary documents to export as well as the corresponding manufacturing quality certificates.

    Product Sample: we send the sample to the customer via DHL; If the client prefers, we make a video call via Skype, wechat, whatsapp, so that the client can observe the product in question in detail.

    Production processes and finished product: Through visits to the factory during the manufacturing process we closely monitor the product before it is delivered as a finished product, thus avoiding any type of anomaly.

    Shipping logistics and documentation

    We verify that the merchandise is properly packaged and labeled so that it arrives in good condition to be dispatched in the correct time and place.

    Correctly documented to satisfy national and foreign government requirements, as well as collection regulations: insured against damage, loss and theft.

    We take care of verifying the original BL shipping documents, as well as sending this document to the client to make a claim for their merchandise at the destination port.

    We prepare the commercial invoice and verify the packing list, to avoid delays that frequently occur at destination customs when receiving goods that are poorly documented.

    Visits to Fairs and business trips

    We advise our clients on their business trips, visits to factories and trade fairs; We plan and value your complete plan including transportation and lodging costs within the Chinese territory, as well as providing the translation service from Chinese to Spanish and or English to Spanish.
